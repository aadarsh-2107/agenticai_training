# Human Resources Policy

**Company:** Acme Corporation
**Owner:** People & Culture Team
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01
**Version:** 1.0
**Applies To:** All full-time and part-time employees

---

## 1. Purpose
This policy sets out the standards, rights, and responsibilities that govern the
employment relationship at Acme Corporation. It aims to ensure fair, consistent,
and lawful treatment of all employees.

## 2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees, interns, and fixed-term staff. Independent
contractors are covered only where explicitly stated.

## 3. Working Hours & Attendance
- Standard working week is **40 hours**, Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
- Core collaboration hours are **10:00 AM to 4:00 PM**.
- Employees must record attendance via the HR timekeeping system daily.
- Flextime is available with manager approval.

## 4. Leave & Time Off

| Leave Type        | Entitlement (per year) | Notes                                  |
|-------------------|------------------------|----------------------------------------|
| Paid Vacation     | 20 days                | Accrues monthly; max carryover 5 days  |
| Sick Leave        | 10 days                | Medical note required after 3 days     |
| Parental Leave    | 16 weeks               | Paid at 100% for primary caregiver     |
| Bereavement Leave | 5 days                 | Immediate family                       |
| Public Holidays   | 11 days                | Per national calendar                  |

- Leave requests must be submitted at least **2 weeks in advance** except in emergencies.
- Unused sick leave does not carry over.

## 5. Code of Conduct
- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and integrity.
- Harassment, bullying, and discrimination of any kind are strictly prohibited.
- Conflicts of interest must be disclosed to HR promptly.
- Company assets must be used responsibly and for business purposes.

## 6. Anti-Harassment & Equal Opportunity
Acme is an equal-opportunity employer. Discrimination based on race, gender, age,
religion, disability, or any protected characteristic is prohibited. Complaints may
be reported confidentially to HR at **hr@acme.example**, and **no retaliation** will
be tolerated against anyone who reports in good faith.

## 7. Compensation & Benefits
- Salaries are paid monthly on the **last business day** of the month.
- Annual performance reviews determine merit increases and bonuses.
- Benefits include health insurance, a retirement plan with 4% match, and an
  employee assistance program (EAP).

## 8. Performance Management
- Formal reviews occur **twice per year** (mid-year and year-end).
- Underperformance may result in a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP) of 60 days.

## 9. Remote & Hybrid Work
- Eligible employees may work remotely up to **3 days per week**.
- Employees must maintain a secure work environment and be reachable during core hours.

## 10. Termination & Offboarding
- Employees must provide **4 weeks written notice** of resignation.
- Company assets must be returned on the last working day.
- Exit interviews are conducted by HR.

## 11. Roles & Responsibilities
- **Employees:** Comply with policy and report concerns.
- **Managers:** Apply policy fairly and support their teams.
- **HR:** Maintain, communicate, and enforce this policy.

## 12. Compliance & Consequences
Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination,
consistent with applicable law.
