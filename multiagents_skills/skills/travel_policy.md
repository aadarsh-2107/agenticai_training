# Business Travel & Expense (T&E) Policy

**Company:** Acme Corporation
**Owner:** Finance & Operations Team
**Effective Date:** 2026-01-01
**Version:** 1.0
**Applies To:** All employees traveling on company business

---

## 1. Purpose
This policy defines the rules for booking, undertaking, and expensing business
travel to ensure costs are reasonable, necessary, and properly documented.

## 2. Scope
Applies to all domestic and international travel undertaken for business purposes
by Acme employees.

## 3. General Principles
All travel must be **reasonable, necessary, and business-justified**. Employees
should select the lowest logical cost option consistent with safety and efficiency.

## 4. Trip Approval

| Trip Cost (USD)     | Approver                 |
|---------------------|--------------------------|
| Up to $1,000        | Direct Manager           |
| $1,001 – $5,000     | Department Head          |
| Over $5,000         | VP / Finance Director    |

- Pre-approval is required **before** booking any travel.

## 5. Booking Procedures
- All travel must be booked via the approved travel tool **AcmeTravel Portal**.
- Flights should be booked at least **14 days in advance** where possible.

## 6. Air Travel

| Flight Duration     | Cabin Class     |
|---------------------|-----------------|
| Under 6 hours       | Economy         |
| 6 hours or more     | Premium Economy |
| Over 10 hours       | Business (VP+)  |

- Personal upgrades are at the employee's own expense.
- Loyalty program points earned on business travel may be retained by the employee.

## 7. Ground Transportation
- **Mileage reimbursement** for personal vehicle use: **$0.65 per mile**.
- Rental cars: economy or intermediate class; decline optional insurance where
  corporate coverage applies.
- Rideshare/taxi permitted for reasonable business trips.

## 8. Lodging

| City Tier   | Nightly Cap (USD) |
|-------------|-------------------|
| Tier 1      | $300              |
| Tier 2      | $220              |
| Tier 3      | $160              |

- Standard rooms only; preferred hotels should be used where available.

## 9. Meals & Incidentals (Per Diem)

| Region        | Daily Per Diem (USD) |
|---------------|----------------------|
| Domestic      | $75                  |
| International | $100                 |

- Alcohol is **not reimbursable** except for approved client entertainment.

## 10. Non-Reimbursable Expenses
- Personal entertainment, minibar, spa, and in-room movies.
- Traffic/parking fines.
- Costs for accompanying family members.
- Loss/theft of personal items.

## 11. Expense Reporting
- Submit expense reports via the **AcmeExpense** tool within **30 days** of travel.
- Receipts required for any expense **over $25**.
- Manager approval required before reimbursement.

## 12. International Travel
- Ensure valid passport, visas, and required vaccinations.
- Travel insurance is mandatory and arranged by the company.
- Register itinerary with the duty-of-care travel tracker.

## 13. Duty of Care & Safety
Acme monitors traveler safety. Employees must register trips and provide emergency
contact details. Travel to high-risk destinations requires additional approval.

## 14. Policy Violations
Non-compliant expenses may be denied reimbursement, and repeated violations may
result in disciplinary action.
